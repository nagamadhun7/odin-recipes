Egg Fried Rice

Description

This egg fried rice recipe is the best! It tastes just like take-out and is ready to serve in a flash.

Ingredients

  1. 4 teaspoons canola oil, divided
  2. 2 large eggs, beaten
  3. 2 carrots, chopped
  4. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  5. 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  6. 1 cup frozen peas
  7. 4 scallions, minced
  8. 4 cups chilled cooked rice
  9. 1 teaspoon soy sauce, or to taste
